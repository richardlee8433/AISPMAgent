MentorFlow v0.7 is live — and this version adds something special: your own knowledge. (Try it : https://lnkd.in/eQe_DJ2K) For the first time, MentorFlow can learn from the user. v0.7 introduces a lightweight RAG pipeline that allows you to upload documents and get traceable, citation-based answers. (This is also what every B2B customer asks for.) For an AI learning assistant, this is a huge step. (And honestly, I’m excited, too!) It moves MentorFlow from “an interactive lesson bot” toward “a personalized teaching system that understands your materials.” Here’s what’s new in v0.7: 1. Knowledge Upload (TXT / PDF v1) You can now upload your own notes or training material. MentorFlow automatically chunk-splits the content, embeds it, and stores it as a mini knowledge base. Perfect for: internal training onboarding documents PMP study notes your own course scripts 2. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) MentorFlow now answers strictly based on retrieved context. Each answer ends with a small citation block like: 📚 Sources Lesson 4.2 – Projects drive change file1_chunk_03 (lines 200–350) This makes the system more transparent and more trustworthy. 3. Streaming Responses + “Retrieving…” indicator To handle retrieval latency, v0.7 switches to a streaming UI. Answers start typing immediately, while citations appear at the end. A small “🔎 Retrieving relevant content…” message appears during lookups to improve perceived responsiveness. 4. Evaluation: Golden Dataset I built a small 20-question evaluation set combining default content and uploaded docs. This lets me measure: hit rate accuracy token cost delta improvements to Session Depth (my North Star metric) 5. RAG On/Off Toggle (Admin mode) For debugging and A/B testing. This helps compare LLM-only vs RAG-grounded answers. v0.7 is the first version where MentorFlow feels like a real AI teaching assistant with memory, not just a lesson engine.
