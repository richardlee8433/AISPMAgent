“It Works” Isn’t Enough — How PMs Decide When to Say NO  
  
Most people assume that if an AI system “works,” it’s ready to ship.  
  
But in real AI product management, working ≠ worth taking responsibility for.  
  
The key question isn’t: Does it function?  
It’s: Would I say YES to this if the consequences landed on me?  
  
✅ A model can be correct, fast, and impressive  
yet still fail at the trust boundaries that matter.  
  
A responsible PM evaluates the risk of every outcome, not just the output.  
Saying NO isn’t about slowing down; it’s about ensuring the system behaves responsibly before release.  
  
This is the core difference between quality and accountable decision-making.
