The “3 Lines Learning System” I’m using to grow into an AI PM

Trying to go deeper into AI as a PM has been overwhelming.

Very quickly, I realized a few things.

You can’t learn AI only by watching tutorials.
Most of them become outdated within a week.

You can’t learn AI only by reading papers.
There are far too many, even with tools like Google’s LLM Notebook.

And you can’t learn AI only by building features.
Without understanding the “why”, it becomes random coding.

So I designed a learning system that actually works for how I learn.
And I’m building it in public through MentorFlow.

AI is moving too fast.
If I wait until I “fully understand everything,” I won’t start at all.

These three lines create a learning loop.
Build, so my understanding grows.
Write, so my reflection sharpens.
Learn the theory so each new iteration is better than the last.

This is how I’m growing into the kind of AI PM I want to be —
not by reading hundreds of papers,
but by combining hands-on work, theory, and reflection.
