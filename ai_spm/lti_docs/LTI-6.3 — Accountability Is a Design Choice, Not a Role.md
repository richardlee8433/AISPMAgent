Accountability Is a Design Choice, Not a Role  
  
When AI starts influencing actions, accountability isn’t about job titles.  
It’s about system design: who must make the decision, and who carries the consequences.  
  
✅ Accountability is baked into the process, not the person’s badge.  
In practice:  
  
• One system-level decision can prevent countless downstream errors.  
• Assigning ownership isn’t bureaucracy — it’s enforcing responsibility where it matters.  
• Abstract governance frameworks don’t protect anyone if the decisions aren’t clearly owned.  
  
A PM who designs accountability into the workflow ensures:  
  
• Every critical outcome has a responsible decision owner  
• Evidence and metrics feed the decisions they can actually influence  
• The team can trust that AI is behaving within safe, observable boundaries  
  
This is the difference between passive oversight and active, auditable governance.
